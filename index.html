Rebates

What the federal rebate means for your system.

Most of the upfront saving on a NSW solar or battery install comes from the federal Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme, not a separate state rebate. Here's how it works in plain terms.

Applied at quote time

The discount is taken off your system price upfront — you don't apply for it or wait for a refund.

Based on system size

Larger systems generate more certificates, which means a bigger discount on the total cost.

Batteries included

Battery installs paired with solar can also qualify for a separate upfront discount.

Reduces over time

The scheme phases down each year until it ends in 2030, so earlier installs get a larger discount.
